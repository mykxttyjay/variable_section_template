About Us – LinktoThrive
LinktoThrive partners with small local businesses that are invisible, lacking exposure, or just trying to get off the ground, and helps them become seen, considered, and remembered by the right customers. We blend indoor digital billboards, local cross‑promotion, and foundational digital marketing to create practical visibility systems that fit real‑world businesses.
The simple slogan we live by is: Connect. Share. Thrive.
We believe that when businesses are truly connected, when they share each other’s stories and exposure, everyone grows and thrives together.

Our Mission
Our mission is to help small local businesses connect, share, and thrive together.
Connect – We create links between your business, your ideal customers, and other local businesses around you. That means being present where people already eat, play, and shop, and making sure your brand shows up consistently across screens and online touchpoints.
Share – We help you tell your story in a way that feels authentic and relevant, then share it across a network of local locations and digital channels. Your strengths, values, and personality shouldn’t be hidden; they should be shared with the community you serve.
Thrive – When your story connects with the right people and your value is clearly seen and appreciated, results follow: stronger awareness, more foot traffic, better word‑of‑mouth, and a community that rallies around its local businesses. Our mission is to support that kind of growth, not just one‑time visibility.

Founder Bio – Davy Nget
Davy Nget is the founder and CEO of LinktoThrive, a relationship‑driven marketing company focused on helping small local businesses become seen, trusted, and chosen in their communities. He brings a blend of practical problem‑solving, marketing strategy, and genuine care for owners to every engagement.
Over his career as product development and marketing manager, Davy has seen that great products and services aren’t enough by themselves. Success happens when people know about them, understand them, and trust the business behind them. He has spent over two decades working at the intersection of what customers need and what businesses offer, focusing on how to communicate value clearly, connect solutions to real problems, and position offerings where the right people will see and appreciate them.
Looking ahead, Davy’s vision is to build a connected network of small businesses that actively share each other’s stories and exposure. Instead of marketing in isolation, he wants local owners to benefit from being linked together so that when one business grows, the whole community moves forward.
